About

Teamfight Tactics is a popular auto-battler game mode in League of Legends. It offers a strategic and competitive gameplay experience where players build teams of champions and deploy them on a chess-like board. The goal is to outsmart and outmaneuver opponents by strategically positioning units and leveraging synergies between different champions and items.
